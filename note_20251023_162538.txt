Peacock, yaani mor, ek aisa khoobsurat pakshi hai jise dekhkar har koi dang reh jaata hai. Iski chamakdaar aur rang-birangi pankh, khaaskar jab ye naachta hai, toh lagta hai jaise dharti par koi kala ka adbhut namuna utar aaya ho. Bharat mein mor ko rashtriya pakshi ka darja mila hua hai, aur iska ek khaas sthan hai hamari sanskriti aur parampara mein.

Mor ki sabse khaas pehchan uske lambe, iridescent pankh hote hain jin par "eyes" yaani aankh jaise design bane hote hain. Jab mor apne pankh failata hai, toh woh ek bade fan ki tarah dikhte hain, jise dekhkar mann khush ho jaata hai. Ye pankh sirf khoobsurti ke liye hi nahi, balki mating season mein female mor (peahen) ko attract karne ke liye bhi hote hain.

Mor junglon aur khet-khaliyan mein paaye jaate hain. Ye sarvahaari hote hain, yaani keede-makode, saap, chote janwar, aur daane bhi khaate hain. Inki awaaz thodi teekhi aur buland hoti hai, jise sunkar kabhi-kabhi log chaunk jaate hain.

Hamari lok kathaaon, kavitaon aur kala mein mor ka ek alag hi mahatva hai. Ise shubhta, saundarya aur garv ka prateek mana jaata hai. Kai devi-devtaon se bhi iska sambandh joda gaya hai, jaise Bhagwan Krishna apne mukut mein mor pankh lagate hain.

Lekin afsos ki baat hai ki shikar aur paryavaran mein badlav ke karan mor ki sankhya kam ho rahi hai. Hamein is khoobsurat pakshi ko bachane ke liye kadam uthane honge taaki aane wali peedhiyan bhi iski sundarta ka anand le sakein. Mor sirf ek pakshi nahi, balki prakriti ka ek anmol tohfa hai jise sanjo kar rakhna hamari zimmedari hai.
